Cremorne Smart Pole Network
By accessing and using the free public Wi-Fi network in Cremorne, you agree to the following terms and conditions:
1. Purpose and Access
  • This free public Wi-Fi service is provided by the City of Yarra via smart pole infrastructure and operated by a service provider.
  • The service is available in selected locations across Cremorne, via smart pole infrastructure.
  • No registration or login is required to access the network.
  • The service is intended for general public and business use including web browsing, email, online learning, and accessing community and government resources.
2. User Responsibilities and Acceptable Use
  • You must use the service legally, responsibly, and respectfully.
  • You must not:
    • Use the service for unlawful, offensive, or malicious purposes
    • Access or share material that is illegal, pornographic, violent, discriminatory, or breaches copyright
    • Attempt to bypass network restrictions or interfere with its functionality
    • Consume excessive bandwidth to the detriment of other users
  • Examples of prohibited activity:
    • Hacking, phishing, or spreading malware
    • Streaming or downloading large media files that exceed fair use
    • Disruptive online behaviour affecting others
3. Download Limit
  • To maintain equitable access:
    • A 1GB download limit per session applies
    • After this limit is reached, your session may slow down or disconnect
4. Security and Privacy
  • This is an open and unsecured network. The City of Yarra does not guarantee the security of any data transmitted.
  • You are advised to:
    • Use secure (HTTPS) websites
    • Avoid transmitting personal or financial data
    • Use a VPN (Virtual Private Network) where possible
5. Content Disclaimer
  • The City of Yarra does not control the internet content accessible through this service.
  • Users are solely responsible for the websites and content they access.
  • The internet may contain offensive, misleading, or inappropriate material. Use caution and discretion.
6. Data Collection and Network Reporting
  • To measure the performance of the public Wi-Fi, anonymised technical data and analytics may be collected and reported.
  • Data Reports May Include:
    1. Real-time or monthly usage reports
    2. Reports confirming network compliance with service specifications
    3. Network usage analytics
    4. Basic and advanced end-user centric analytics (aggregated and anonymised)
  • Metrics Collected May Include:
    • Device types and protocols used
    • Number and duration of sessions
    • Number of unique Wi-Fi clients
    • Data volume per session and access point
    • Total data transferred (daily/monthly)
    • Access point traffic volume
    • Active/inactive access point status
    • Geo-location of access points
    • Access point rankings by user count and data use
  • All data is handled in accordance with the City of Yarra Privacy Policy and the Privacy and Data Protection Act 2014 (Vic). No personal information is required to use the service.
7. Service Availability and Limitations
  • The Wi-Fi service is provided on an "as is" and "as available" basis.
  • Performance may vary due to demand, location, and technical issues.
  • Access may be restricted temporarily for maintenance or upgrades.
8. Liability Disclaimer
  • The City of Yarra or its service providers does not accept responsibility for:
    • Loss or corruption of data
    • Security risks to devices
    • Exposure to inappropriate online content
    • Service disruptions or technical faults
    • Any loss or damage resulting from use of the Wi-Fi service
  • Use of this service is entirely at your own risk.
9. Changes to Terms
These terms may be updated at any time. Continued use of the service after any changes will constitute your acceptance of the revised terms.
